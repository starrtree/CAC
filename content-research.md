## Core content direction

The website should communicate CAC as a high-trust commercial mechanical contractor with deep Cincinnati history, not a generic residential HVAC company.

Key themes:

- Commercial HVAC and mechanical construction
- Design-build delivery
- Plan/spec construction
- Service and preventive maintenance
- In-house engineering capability
- Safety leadership
- Sheet metal, piping, controls, and field execution
- 24-hour service support
- Start-to-finish project partnership
- Long regional history since 1938

- Careers

## Tone

Professional, premium, grounded, capable, and modern. Avoid trendy SaaS aesthetics that feel disconnected from mechanical construction. The design should feel like a serious contractor with engineering discipline and enough polish to beat a standard agency template.

## Visual direction

- Deep navy and clean white base
- CAC red as the action color
- CAC blue as engineering / trust color
- Subtle blueprint grid and mechanical-system visual language
- Rounded cards and clean section rhythm
- Strong typography and clear CTA hierarchy
- Mobile-first responsiveness
- Lightweight animations only
