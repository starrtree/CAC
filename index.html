The Cincinnati Air story

A Cincinnati engineering legacy since 1938.

Arthur Radtke’s persistence became the foundation of The Cincinnati Air Conditioning Company. From early Carrier systems and wartime manufacturing support to design-build HVAC, Kool Oil, Thermolinear environmental chambers, and modern engineered solutions, CAC has endured by embracing changes in the HVAC industry and leading through them.
